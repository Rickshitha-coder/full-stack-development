Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI technologies are used in various fields such as healthcare, education, banking, transportation, and entertainment. Applications like virtual assistants, recommendation systems, language translation, and self-driving vehicles rely on AI to improve efficiency and accuracy.

Artificial Intelligence vs Human Intelligence

Features of Artificial Intelligence

  1. Processes large amounts of data quickly.
  2. Works continuously without fatigue.
  3. Provides high accuracy in repetitive tasks.
  4. Can automate complex processes.
  5. Learns from data using machine learning algorithms.

Features of Human Intelligence

  • Possesses creativity and imagination.
  • Can understand emotions and empathy.
  • Makes decisions based on experience and intuition.
  • Adapts to new situations naturally.
  • Exhibits moral and ethical reasoning.
